# Tariq Shaikh | Product Portfolio

A collection of strategic tools and applications built at the intersection of data, engineering, and product management. These projects represent real-world solutions to personal and professional challenges.

## Featured Applications

### 1. Orbit: Cash Flow Intelligence
**Strategic Wealth Simulator**
Orbit is a "What-If" engine for your financial future. It helps you project your financial trajectory based on real inflow and outflow, allowing you to visualize the impact of life's big decisions—from daycare and new cars to long-term investments.
- **Key Features**: Annual surplus mapping, irregular expense "orbit" tracking, sinking fund automation, and AI-driven financial coaching.
- **Tech**: React, Firebase (Auth/Firestore), Recharts, Gemini AI.

### 2. Homebase NJ
**The Ultimate Homebuyer's Decision Tool**
Designed for prospective residents to compare New Jersey municipalities across multiple data dimensions like schools, commute, safety, and affordability.
- **Key Features**: Dynamic priority weighting, side-by-side town comparison matrix, and live verified data from all 21 NJ counties.
- **Tech**: React, Tailwind CSS, Motion, Lucide.

### 3. Waves: Dream Trip Planner
**Destination Intelligence**
Your personal oracle for dream trip planning. Discover exactly when you should go, what's happening locally, and how to make that one specific bucket-list journey a reality.
- **Key Features**: Price drop alerts, historical trend charts, multi-user collaboration, and AI-powered seasonality insights.
- **Tech**: React, Recharts, Gemini AI, Tailwind CSS.

### 4. Future Projects
- **Data Strategy Dashboard**: A tool for visualizing complex market trends and A/B test results.
- **Product Roadmap Planner**: A streamlined interface for managing 0→1 product launches and agile sprints.
