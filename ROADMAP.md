# Product Roadmap: Orbit

## V1 — Strategic Core (Current: Q1 2026)
### Wealth Pulse Dashboard
- **Real-time Net Worth:** Accurate calculation based on categorized assets and liabilities. (Done)
- **Auto-Save:** Instant synchronization of ledger entries to ensure data integrity without manual intervention. (Done)
- **Multi-Currency Support:** Support for 15+ major global currencies with real-time conversion capabilities. (Done)
- **Historical Performance:** Track your wealth accumulation over months, quarters, and years. (In Progress)
- **Retirement Planner:** Comprehensive retirement modeling, cash flow projections, and scenario analysis similar to Root Financial's workflow. (In Progress)

### What-If Engine
- Interactive simulation of major life events (house, career, kids). (Planned)
- Opportunity Cost Feed: Translating daily spend into future potential wealth. (Planned)

## V2 — Intelligence (Q2 2026)
### AI Advisory
- **Gemini Wealth Coach:** Personalized strategic advice based on scenario analysis. (Discovery)
- **Tax Optimization:** Simulating tax-efficient withdrawal and contribution strategies. (Discovery)

### Statement Analyzer
- **Granular Insights:** Upload your debit and credit card statements to get granular insights into your monthly spending and usage patterns. (Planned)

## V3 — Scaling (Q3 2026)
### Advanced Features
- **Household Budget Input:** Input income, down payment, and rates to filter out towns outside true affordability. (Discovery)
- **Live MLS / Zillow API:** Replace static median prices with real-time active inventory and list prices. (Planned)
- **User Accounts & Auth:** Allow users to save multiple comparison scenarios and share them with partners. (Planned)

## V4 — Vision (Q4 2026+)
### National & Monetization
- **US National Expansion:** Scale the data pipeline to support the top 50 US Metropolitan Statistical Areas (MSAs). (Backlog)
- **Predictive Insights:** AI-driven insights on bidding wars, average days on market, and offer strategies. (Backlog)
- **Agent Lead Gen Engine:** Monetization: Connect high-intent buyers with top-rated local real estate agents. (Backlog)
