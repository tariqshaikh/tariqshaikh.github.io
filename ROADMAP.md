# Product Roadmap: Orbit

## V1 — Strategic Core (Current: Q1 2026)
### Annual Finance Orbit
- **Orbit Expense Engine:** Core logic for tracking irregular, non-monthly expenses and their annual impact. (Done)
- **Fluid Expense Grid:** Real-time dashboard showing annual income, spend, surplus, and categorized irregular hits. (Done)
- **Dynamic Fixed Expenses:** Customizable monthly fixed expenses to accurately calculate baseline spend. (Done)
- **Color Customization:** Allow users to personalize the visual dashboard with custom colors. (Done)

## V2 — Intelligence (Q2 2026)
### AI Advisory
- **Gemini Wealth Coach:** Personalized strategic advice based on annual cash flow analysis. (Done)
- **Statement Analyzer:** Granular insights from uploaded financial statements to auto-detect orbiting expenses. (Planned)

## V3 — Wealth & Surplus (Q3 2026)
### Surplus Deployment
- **Surplus Ideas:** Actionable strategies for deploying the "Annual Surplus" into investments, high-yield savings, or other wealth-building vehicles. (Discovery)
- **Wealth Simulator:** (In Production) Comprehensive retirement modeling, cash flow projections, and scenario analysis. (In Progress)

## V4 — Vision (Q4 2026+)
### Advanced Planning
- **Tax Optimization:** Simulating tax-efficient withdrawal and contribution strategies. (Backlog)
- **Opportunity Cost Feed:** Translating daily/monthly spend into future potential wealth. (Backlog)
